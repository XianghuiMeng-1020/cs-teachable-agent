### Task: Hero's Quest

In a mythical realm, heroes embark on quests to gain divine favor. As a programmer tasked with analyzing these quests, your goal is to determine the best strategy for a hero to gain maximum divine favor when presented with a series of challenges.

#### Function to Implement:
Create a function `maximize_favor(challenges)` that accepts a list of tuples, where each tuple contains two integers: `(reward, difficulty)`. These integers represent the divine reward and the difficulty of each challenge that a hero must face.

The hero can select challenges such that:
1. The sum total of difficulty does not exceed 10 (this is the hero's maximum endurance).
2. The goal is to maximize the total divine reward.

Return the maximum divine reward possible without surpassing a total difficulty of 10.

#### Example:
Given the list of challenges:
```
challenges = [(3, 2), (5, 4), (7, 6), (1, 1)]
```
The function `maximize_favor(challenges)` should return `8`, since the hero can choose the challenges with rewards 7 and 1, having a total difficulty of 6+1 = 7, which is under the limit of 10.

#### Additional Constraints:
- The list of challenges will have at least one and at most ten items.

Implement the function such that it operates efficiently within these constraints.
