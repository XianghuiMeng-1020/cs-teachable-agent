```
Earth 15000
Mars 12000
```

- `output_file`: a string representing the filepath to where the total energy consumption will be written.

Your task is to ensure that the total energy consumption is calculated correctly and that it appears as a single number in the provided output file. You are guaranteed that the input file format is valid and does not have any empty lines or invalid data.
